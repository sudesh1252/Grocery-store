  SPECIFIC CHANGES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. BACKGROUND
   Before: bg-gradient-to-br from-primary-50 to-primary-100
   After:  bg-gradient-to-br from-emerald-50 via-teal-50 to-cyan-50
   
   Why: More modern, professional, and visually appealing

2. CARD DESIGN
   Before: bg-white rounded-2xl shadow-xl
   After:  bg-white/80 backdrop-blur-sm rounded-3xl shadow-2xl border border-white/20
   
   Why: Glassmorphism is trendy and looks premium

3. LOGO
   Before: w-16 h-16 bg-primary-500 rounded-2xl shadow-lg
   After:  w-20 h-20 bg-gradient-to-br from-emerald-500 to-teal-600 rounded-3xl shadow-2xl
           + transform hover:scale-110 transition-transform duration-300
   
   Why: Larger, more prominent, with engaging animation

4. TITLE
   Before: text-3xl font-bold text-gray-800
   After:  text-4xl font-bold bg-gradient-to-r from-emerald-600 to-teal-600 
           bg-clip-text text-transparent
   
   Why: Gradient text is eye-catching and modern

5. SUBTITLE
   Before: text-gray-600
   After:  text-gray-600 text-lg
   
   Why: Better readability

6. BUTTON
   Before: btn btn-primary py-3
   After:  bg-gradient-to-r from-emerald-500 to-teal-600 py-3.5 rounded-xl
           shadow-lg hover:shadow-xl transform hover:scale-[1.02]
   
   Why: More engaging, better feedback

7. DEMO CREDENTIALS BOX
   Before: bg-blue-50 border border-blue-200 rounded-lg
   After:  bg-gradient-to-r from-blue-50 to-indigo-50 border border-blue-200 
           rounded-2xl shadow-lg
   
   Why: Consistent with overall design
