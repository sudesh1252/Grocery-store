## Getting Started

### First Time Login

1. **Open the Application**
   - Open your web browser
   - Go to: `http://localhost:3000`

2. **Create Your Account**
   - Click "Sign Up" button
   - Enter your full name
   - Enter your email address
   - Create a strong password
   - Click "Create Account"

3. **Login**
   - Enter your email
   - Enter your password
   - Click "Login"

1. **Dashboard** - Overview of your store
2. **Billing** - Create new invoices
3. **History** - View past invoices

## Billing System

### Creating an Invoice

#### Step 1: Add Products

1. Go to **Billing** page
2. Use the search box to find products
3. Type product name or SKU
4. Click on the product to add it
5. Product appears in the cart

#### Step 2: Adjust Quantities

1. Find the product in your cart
2. Click on the quantity field
3. Enter the desired quantity
4. Price updates automatically

#### Step 3: Enter Customer Details

1. Scroll to customer information section
2. Enter customer name (required)
3. Enter phone number (optional)

#### Step 4: Review & Generate

1. Check all items and quantities
2. Review subtotal, tax, and total
3. Click "Generate Invoice"
4. Invoice is created and saved

#### Step 5: Print Invoice

1. After generation, print dialog opens
2. Select your printer
3. Click "Print"
4. Invoice is printed

### Tips for Fast Billing

- **Use Search**: Type product name quickly
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Use Tab to move between fields
- **Quick Quantity**: Click quantity field and type number
- **Remove Items**: Click red X button to remove
- **Clear All**: Click "Clear Cart" to start fresh

### Understanding the Cart

**Product Information:**
- Product name
- Unit price
- Quantity selector
- Total price per item

**Summary:**
- Subtotal (before tax)
- Tax (GST %)
- Total Amount (final)

## Inventory Management

### Viewing Products

**Product Table Shows:**
- Product Name & Unit
- SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)
- Category
- Cost Price (purchase)
- Selling Price (retail)
- Current Stock
- Total Value

**Stock Indicators:**
- 🟢 **Green** - Good stock (above minimum)
- 🟡 **Yellow** - Low stock (at or below minimum)
- 🔴 **Red** - Out of stock (zero)

### Adding a New Product

1. Click "Add Product" button
2. Fill in the form:

**Required Fields:**
- Product Name
- SKU (unique code)
- Category (select from dropdown)
- Selling Price
- Stock Quantity

**Optional Fields:**
- Barcode
- Purchase Price
- Minimum Stock Alert
- Description

3. Click "Add Product"
4. Product appears in table

### Editing a Product

1. Find the product in table
2. Click blue **Edit** button
3. Modify any fields
4. Click "Update Product"
5. Changes are saved

### Deleting a Product

1. Find the product in table
2. Click red **Delete** button
3. Confirm deletion
4. Product is removed

### Using Filters

**Show Filters Panel:**
- Click "Filters" button

**Filter Options:**
1. **Category** - Select specific category
2. **Stock Status**:
   - All Stock
   - Good Stock
   - Low Stock
   - Out of Stock
3. **Sort By**:
   - Name
   - Price
   - Stock
   - Category
4. **Sort Order**:
   - Ascending (↑)
   - Descending (↓)

### Exporting Inventory

1. Apply any filters (optional)
2. Click "Export" button
3. CSV file downloads
4. Open in Excel/Google Sheets

**CSV Includes:**
- All product details
- Current stock levels
- Values and prices

### Understanding Statistics

**Dashboard Cards:**
- **Total Products** - Count of all items
- **Low Stock** - Items needing reorder
- **Out of Stock** - Items unavailable
- **Total Value** - Worth of all inventory

## Reports & Analytics

### Types of Reports

1. **Daily Report** - Hourly breakdown
2. **Monthly Report** - Daily breakdown
3. **Yearly Report** - Monthly breakdown

### Viewing Reports

#### Daily Report

1. Click "Daily" button
2. Select date from calendar
3. View hourly sales chart
4. See total sales and invoices

**Shows:**
- Sales for each hour
- Number of bills per hour
- Total daily revenue
- Total daily invoices

#### Monthly Report

1. Click "Monthly" button
2. Select month from dropdown
3. Select year
4. View daily sales chart

**Shows:**
- Sales for each day
- Number of bills per day
- Total monthly revenue
- Total monthly invoices

#### Yearly Report

1. Click "Yearly" button
2. Select year
3. View monthly sales chart

**Shows:**
- Sales for each month
- Number of bills per month
- Total yearly revenue
- Total yearly invoices

### Understanding Charts

**Bar Charts:**
- Each bar represents a time period
- Height shows sales amount
- Color indicates report type:
  - Blue/Cyan - Daily
  - Indigo/Purple - Monthly
  - Teal/Green - Yearly

**Summary Cards:**
- Total Sales
- Total Invoices
- Average Sale per invoice

### Printing Reports

1. View the report you want
2. Click "Print" button
3. Print preview opens
4. Select printer
5. Click "Print"

**Print Shows:**
- Store name
- Report title and date
- All statistics
- Charts and tables
- Recent invoices

### Exporting Reports

## Common Questions

### Q: How do I change my password?
A: Go to Profile → Settings → Change Password

### Q: Can I edit an invoice after creation?
A: No, invoices are final. Create a new invoice if needed.

### Q: How do I handle returns?
A: Create a new invoice with negative quantities (coming soon)

### Q: Can multiple users use the system?
A: Yes, each user needs their own account

### Q: How do I backup my data?
A: Export inventory regularly and backup database

### Q: What if I make a mistake?
A: Most actions can be undone. Contact support if needed.
