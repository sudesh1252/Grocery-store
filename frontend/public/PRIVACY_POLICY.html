1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you purchase or register for our Software, we collect:

  • Contact Information:
    • Full Name
    • Business Name (Store Name)
    • Email Address
    • Phone Number / WhatsApp Number

  • Payment Information:

    • IP Address

  • Usage Data:

    • License activation date
    • Support requests and communications
    • Software version being used

Important: Your Business Data is Private!

We DO NOT collect, access, or store:

  • ❌ Your customer information

  • ❌ Your sales transactions or invoices
  • ❌ Your financial reports or revenue data
  • ❌ Your employee information
  • ❌ Any data you enter into the Software

All your business data is stored locally on your computer/server and remains completely private.

4.1 Where We Store Your Data

  • Personal Information: Stored securely on cloud servers in India/Singapore
  • Payment Information: Stored by payment gateways (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Your Business Data: Stored locally on YOUR computer/server (we don't have access)

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • 🔒 SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • 🔒 Encrypted database storage
  • 🔒 Secure access controls and authentication
  • 🔒 Regular security audits and updates
  • 🔒 Firewall protection
  • 🔒 Limited employee access to personal data

4.3 Data Retention

  • Active Customers: Data retained as long as you use our Software
